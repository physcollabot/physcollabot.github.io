Prior Experience

Senior organizers bring substantial workshop leadership

Vijay Kumar

Prof. Vijay Kumar has organized many workshops and conferences, including the 2024 IROS workshop, the 2025 and 2026 ICRA workshops, and multiple editions of the International Symposium on Experimental Robotics. He also served as general co-chair of ICRA 2022 and program chair of ICRA 2010.

Harish Ravichandar

Prof. Harish Ravichandar has successfully organized several workshops, including leading the ICRA 2025 Workshop on Structured Robot Learning.
