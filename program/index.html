Discussion Themes

Panels are structured around concrete technical pressure points

Modeling and abstraction

How should teams model shared loads, contacts, and constraints without losing deployability or computational efficiency?

Long-horizon coordination

What planning structures are needed when manipulation, sequencing, resource conflicts, and execution interact over long horizons?

Learning for physical collaboration

Where can modern data-driven methods help, and what kinds of demonstrations, benchmarks, or sim-to-real pipelines are missing?
